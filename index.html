Who We Are

Investing in Your Financial Future

The CIC Investment Club is a student-led organisation at Columbia International College dedicated to building financial literacy and investment acumen among our members. Whether you're a complete beginner or already following the markets, our club offers a structured, supportive environment to grow.

We believe every student deserves access to financial education. Through weekly training sessions and hands-on simulated trading competitions, we equip members with practical tools they'll carry well beyond their time at CIC.

📚

Weekly Training Sessions

Structured lessons on markets, valuation, macro-economics, and portfolio theory — every week, year-round.

💹

Simulated Stock Competition

Each semester, members compete in a paper-trading competition tracking real U.S. market performance.

🤝

Peer Learning Community

Share ideas, debate investment theses, and collaborate through our shared insights platform and group channels.

Our Mission
  • Democratize financial knowledge for all CIC students
  • Build hands-on experience with real market dynamics
  • Develop critical thinking through investment analysis
  • Foster a collaborative, inclusive investing community
  • Prepare members for future finance careers and studies
  • Bridge the gap between classroom theory and real markets

What We Do

Club Activities

From structured weekly sessions to semester-long competitions, every activity is designed to sharpen your financial edge.

📊
Weekly Training Sessions
Every Week

Our flagship weekly sessions cover a broad curriculum — from foundational concepts like reading a balance sheet, to advanced topics like discounted cash flow analysis and options strategies.

  • 60–90 minute structured lessons each week
  • Guest speakers from finance and business fields
  • Collaborative case studies and market discussion
  • Open to all levels — beginners are very welcome
🏆
Simulated Stock Competition
Each Semester

Each semester, members compete in a paper-trading competition using a virtual portfolio pegged to real U.S. market prices. Build your own portfolio, test your thesis, and compete for the top position.

  • Virtual portfolio starting at $100,000 USD
  • Real-time prices via simulated trading platform
  • Mid-competition strategy reviews and check-ins
  • End-of-semester awards ceremony for top performers
