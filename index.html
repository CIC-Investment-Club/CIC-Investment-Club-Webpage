Who We Are

Building Next-Gen Investors

We're a student-led investment club dedicated to educating and mentoring aspiring investors. Through hands-on experience, collaboration, and rigorous analysis, we prepare members for success in finance.

Founded in 2018, CIC Investment Club has become one of the most prestigious student investment organizations. We combine academic rigor with practical trading experience, giving members an edge in the competitive finance world.

Our members have gone on to lead roles at top investment banks, hedge funds, and fintech startups. We're proud to have nurtured talent that's making waves across the financial industry.

Our Core Activities

From weekly meetings to global competitions, we offer diverse opportunities to develop your investment skills.

📈

Weekly Analysis Sessions

Every Monday

Members present stock picks and market analysis. We discuss fundamental and technical factors, debate valuations, and vote on portfolio additions.

  • Deep dive into company financials
  • Peer feedback and discussion
  • Real-time portfolio decisions
🎓

Educational Workshops

Bi-Weekly

Expert-led seminars covering advanced topics like options trading, macroeconomics, and risk management. Guest speakers from top financial firms.

  • Industry professionals as guest speakers
  • Advanced financial strategies
  • Career insights and networking
🏆

Investment Competitions

3x Per Year

Compete in regional and national investment competitions. Track record of top finishes and cash prizes worth thousands.

  • Regional and national competitions
  • Cash prizes and scholarships
  • Networking with other top clubs
💼

Mentorship Program

Ongoing

One-on-one mentoring with senior members and industry professionals. Guidance on portfolio strategy, career development, and personal growth.

  • Paired with experienced mentors
  • Personalized learning paths
  • Long-term career guidance
