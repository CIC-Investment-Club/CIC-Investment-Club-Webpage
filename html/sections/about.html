Who We Are

Investing in Your Financial Future

The CIC Investment Club is a student-led organisation at Columbia International College dedicated to building financial literacy and investment acumen among our members. Whether you're a complete beginner or already following the markets, our club offers a structured, supportive environment to grow.

We believe every student deserves access to financial education. Through weekly training sessions and hands-on simulated trading competitions, we equip members with practical tools they'll carry well beyond their time at CIC.

📚

Weekly Training Sessions

Structured lessons on markets, valuation, macro-economics, and portfolio theory — every week, year-round.

💹

Simulated Stock Competition

Each semester, members compete in a paper-trading competition tracking real U.S. market performance.

🤝

Peer Learning Community

Share ideas, debate investment theses, and collaborate through our shared insights platform and group channels.

Our Mission
  • Democratise financial knowledge for CIC students
  • Build hands-on experience with real market dynamics
  • Develop critical thinking through investment analysis
  • Foster a collaborative, inclusive investing community
  • Prepare members for managing wealth or future finance careers
  • Bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-life scenarios
