What We Do

Club Activities

From structured weekly sessions to semester-long competitions, every activity is designed to sharpen your financial edge.

📊
Weekly Training Sessions
Every Week

Our flagship weekly sessions cover a broad curriculum — from foundational concepts like reading a balance sheet, to advanced topics like discounted cash flow analysis and options strategies.

  • 60–90 minute structured lessons each week
  • Guest speakers from finance and business fields
  • Collaborative case studies and market discussion
  • Open to all levels — beginners are very welcome
🏆
Simulated Stock Competition
Each Semester

Each semester, members compete in a paper-trading competition using a virtual portfolio pegged to real U.S. market prices. Build your own portfolio, test your thesis, and compete for the top position.

  • Virtual portfolio starting at $100,000 USD
  • Real-time prices via simulated trading platform
  • Mid-competition strategy reviews and check-ins
  • End-of-semester awards ceremony for top performers
